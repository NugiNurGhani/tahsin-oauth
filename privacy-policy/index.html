Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Tahsin app ("Application") for iOS, created by Nugi Nuryanto G ("Service Provider"). By using the Application, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Data We Collect

Tahsin does not operate its own server or database. Most data stays on your device or in your personal iCloud account. Here is what the Application collects and where it goes:

  • Account information (optional). If you sign in with Apple, the Application receives your name and email address from Apple. This information is stored locally on your device and synced to your other Apple devices via iCloud Key-Value Storage.
  • Profile data. Your display name and profile photo are stored locally and synced via iCloud. Your photo is compressed before storage. No one other than you can access your iCloud data.
  • Reading activity. Bookmarks, last-read position, reading streak, and murottal favorites are stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud. This data never leaves Apple's infrastructure unless you connect a Quran Foundation account (see Section 3).
  • Location (optional). The Application requests your location to calculate prayer times and the qibla direction. Your location is used on-device only and is never sent to any external server by the Application.
  • Audio cache. Recitation audio files you download are stored locally in your device's cache folder for offline playback. These files are sourced from third-party recitation providers and are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Device information. Standard iOS system information (iOS version, device model) may be used for debugging purposes. This data is not linked to your identity.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • The Application does not collect your IP address or browsing behavior on any server owned by the Service Provider.
  • The Application does not use advertising networks or sell data to third parties.
  • The Application does not use AI or machine learning to process your personal data, except for the optional on-device AI verse search feature (iOS 26 only), which runs entirely on your device.

3. Quran Foundation Integration (Optional)

You may optionally connect a Quran Foundation account. When connected, the Application sends your daily reading activity (time spent reading, streak data) to the Quran Foundation API to sync your progress across devices and platforms. This connection uses OAuth2 and can be disconnected at any time from Settings → Account. Quran Foundation's own privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy.

4. In-App Purchases

The Application offers optional tips through Apple's in-app purchase system. Payment is processed entirely by Apple. The Service Provider does not receive or store your payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple (iCloud, Sign in with Apple, StoreKit)
  • Quran Foundation (optional, only when connected)

6. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed at children under 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Application, contact us at nughie28@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly. Users must be at least 16 years old to consent to data processing in jurisdictions where that age applies.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Reading data, bookmarks, and profile information stored via iCloud can be deleted by signing out of your Apple ID or deleting the Application. To request deletion of any data held by the Service Provider, contact nughie28@gmail.com. To stop all data collection, uninstall the Application using the standard iOS process.

8. Security

Authentication tokens for connected accounts (Quran Foundation) are stored in the iOS Keychain, which is encrypted by the operating system. iCloud data is protected by Apple's end-to-end encryption where applicable.

10. Contact
